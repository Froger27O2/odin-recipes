Stuffed Pepper Recipe

Description

Homemade stuffed peppers are a pure joy

All the ingredients work well together, composing a beautiful dish

Ingredients

  • Bell Peppers
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Sour Cream
  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Make a sauce using water, sour cream and ketchup
  3. Put rice, meat and tomatoes inside of peppers
  4. Put peppers in the same pan, in which you are preparing the sauce
  5. Cook everything together until ready
